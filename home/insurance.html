Policy Descriptions

Welcome to AgriSure's policy dashboard! We offer a range of policies designed to help farmers like you tackle the challenges posed by climate change. From extreme weather events to heatwaves, our policies ensure you have the support needed to maintain the sustainability of your farm, whether small or large. Explore tailored insurance and subsidy options to protect your farm from environmental uncertainties.

Below is an overview of the various policies we offer, along with pricing and ratings:

  • Climate-Smart Insurance Policy: Protect your farm from extreme weather events such as droughts, floods, and heatwaves. This policy covers crop damage and helps recover from severe weather impacts.
    Pricing: - Small farms: ₹3000/year, Medium farms: ₹700/year, Large farms: ₹1200/year.
    Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.5/5)
  • Drought-Resilience Subsidy Policy: Receive financial assistance to implement drought-resilient farming practices, such as rainwater harvesting and drought-resistant crop varieties.
    Pricing: - Small farms: ₹2000/year, Medium farms: ₹500/year, Large farms: ₹1000/year.
    Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.4/5)
  • Flood Management and Recovery Policy: Mitigate flood risks with preventive strategies and receive recovery assistance in case of damage. This policy supports flood defense mechanisms and compensates for flood-related losses.
    Pricing: - Small farms: ₹2500/year, Medium farms: ₹600/year, Large farms: ₹1100/year.
    Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.8/5)
  • Agroforestry Promotion Policy: Encourage sustainable farming through agroforestry by integrating trees with your crops and livestock. This policy provides grants and training to promote this eco-friendly approach.
    Pricing: - Small farms: ₹1000/year, Medium farms: ₹300/year, Large farms: ₹700/year.
    Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.3/5)
  • Heat-Resilient Farming Policy: Equip your farm with heat-tolerant seeds and cooling systems to combat rising temperatures. This policy is essential for farms in regions prone to heatwaves.
    Pricing: - Small farms: ₹1500/year, Medium farms: ₹400/year, Large farms: ₹800/year.
    Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.5/5)

    Pricing: - Small farms: ₹500/year, Medium farms: ₹150/year, Large farms: ₹300/year.
    Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4.6/5)
